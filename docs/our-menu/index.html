Our Menu

more about the specifics of the menu

A few words...

Living in today's world and knowing all of the trends, the ways of lifestyle and more is really hard and responsible but we, the whole crew from Meal - Prep Kitchen are accepting this challenge knowing that if we make a single mistake, we are the responisble one's for it. By saying "Yes" to this challenge, we are offering you a whole new style of menus for all types of people and all ages. We are the CHANGE in today's world nutritioning!

We are offering you all kind of meats, fish, seafood, fresh and seasonal vegetables and fruits, from which we make the garnish, the salads, the desserts and exactly all meals. We are also happy to offer you a huge range of diary products and eggs coming straight from the BIO farms of Bulgaria. Our range of foods does not stop here! We also have vegetarian, vegan, gluten free meals and we have considered every allergen possible in the making of each and every meal we have on our website!

Even though we offer a meat meals, we trully appreciate every each on of the animals' lives and are trying to avoid killing them. However, all of the animals are born and raised as free animals, never caged or beaten, always given them food and a lot of love!
